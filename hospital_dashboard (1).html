Key Business Insights & Recommendations
⚠️ Unpaid Bills Risk
245 of 500 billing records (49%) remain unpaid. Immediate follow-up protocols and automated payment reminders should be deployed to reduce accounts receivable exposure.
📉 Seasonal Appointment Drop
Appointments drop sharply from April's peak (25) to September's low (11). Consider targeted outreach campaigns in Q3 to maintain patient visit frequency.
🏆 Central Hospital Dominance
Central Hospital generates $229K in treatment revenue — 42% of total. Eastside and Westside clinics are nearly equal. Investigate resource gaps at satellite clinics.
🩺 High Chemotherapy Volume
Chemotherapy leads all treatments (49 cases). Oncology investment and specialist recruitment should be prioritized; long-term oncology patients are high-value retention targets.
👶 Youth & Elderly Underrepresented
Only 1 patient under 20 and 8 over 60. Consider pediatric and geriatric outreach programs to capture underserved segments and diversify the patient portfolio.
🔄 Churn Risk: Low Visit Patients
Several patients appear only once in the dataset. A loyalty program or automated recall system for patients with fewer than 3 annual visits could improve retention significantly.
